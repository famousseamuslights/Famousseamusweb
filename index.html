White Cane Day 2025

Join us in celebrating White Cane Day 2025 at Acacia Park in downtown Colorado Springs on Friday, October 17, 2025! This annual event honors independence, safety, and mobility for individuals who are blind or visually impaired. It’s a day filled with community, music, and inspiration.

🗣️ Event Activities Include:

  • Inspirational speakers and poem readings
  • Three live bands performing throughout the day:
    • 🎺 CSDB Pride Band
    • 🥁 CSDB Bulldog Band
    • 🎷 CSDB Staff Band
  • 🎶 Background music between performances
  • 🍔 Lunch will be served during the event

Don’t miss your chance to meet Seamus and his Aunt June at the Famous Seamus Lights booth — where you can pick up handmade mason-jar lights for just $5 each! All proceeds support the Lions Club and their mission to provide assistive technology for visually impaired individuals.
